Our Mission

To create a User-friendly website.

Our mission is to create a website with a unique approach towards a social issue that everyone can understand. We have made this website to highlight the rising issue of drug abuse, espiecially among the youth. We hope that this website accomplishes it's vison mentioned below.

Our Vision

What we aim for.

Our vision for CONTROLPULSE is to create a comprehensive online platform that serves as a beacon of knowledge, support, and encouragement for individuals grappling with substance abuse issues. Our primary goal is to empower users with information on the detrimental effects of drug abuse while emphasizing proactive prevention measures. Through a user-friendly interface, we aim to provide valuable resources, including educational content, personal narratives, and professional guidance, fostering a sense of community and understanding.
